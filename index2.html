FAQ

What is DASHCOIN.NINJA?

In a nutshell, you send us dashcoins and we make your bitcoin payment.

DASHCOIN.NINJA allows you to make a Bitcoin payment with the strong anonymity provided by Dashcoin. This means that using Dashcoin together with DASHCOIN.NINJA, you can pay any Bitcoin address in the world, anonymously.

How does it work? (short version)

  1. Enter the Bitcoin address you want to pay, and the amount you want to send. Hit the "Create" button.
  2. We tell you how many dashcoins you need to send us.
  3. Once we receive the dashcoins you send us, we immediately make your requested bitcoin payment.

How does it work? (long version)

  1. Create a new order: just enter the Bitcoin address you want to pay, and the precise amount you want to send there.
  2. We set up an order for you. An order is the contract between you and DASHCOIN.NINJA, which states that if we receive a specific number of dashcoins from you, we will make a specific Bitcoin payment on your behalf.
    • Send the amount of dashcoins (DSH) specified in the order to the indicated address, using the payment ID we give you.
    • Please take note of your order secret key that we give you (a unique identifier, 12 characters long, that starts with 'dshninja-…'). If you want to track the status of your order later, you will need this secret key.
    • Your order is only valid for 5 minutes (after which time the exchange rate we quote in the order is out of date). You will need to send us the requested amount of dashcoins within this time window, or we won't be able to process the order. Note that we only need to see the unconfirmed transaction on the p2p network within this time window, confirmation by miners can come later.
    • Don't forget the payment ID when you make the dashcoin payment, or we won't be able to process your payment!
  3. Once we get your Dashcoin payment, we wait for it to be confirmed (typically around one minute), and then immediately send your requested amount of bitcoins to the Bitcoin address that you chose.

Are there any hidden costs?

No. The BTC/DSH exchange rate that you are quoted determines the exact number of dashcoins you need to send us in order to have your Bitcoin payment fulfilled.

How anonymous is DASHCOIN.NINJA?

Dashcoin payments are anonymous. To be fully precise: given the current state of the art in cryptography, your privacy is provably enforced at the mathematical level. Dashcoin allows to make payment over the internet with the strongest privacy currently achievable by cryptocurrencies. That is what we mean by anonymous. Thus no-one (including us) can link you to the payment you send us.

The only other link to us is your IP address (from which you are visiting this webpage right now), which you should hide when you wish to be fully anonymous (see best practices below).

As bitcoin payments are public, payments we make on your behalf will be visible on the (transparent) bitcoin blockchain. However, thanks to the intrinsic properties of Dashcoin, no-one can associate that payment with you.

What are the best practices for privacy?

  • Use TOR or I2P to hide your IP address!
  • Be sure to set a sufficient mixin when sending your Dashcoin payment – we generally recommend 3 or higher.
